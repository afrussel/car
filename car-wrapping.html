AUTOMOTIVE FILM

Film is the most modern way to protect the appearance of a car, make it unique and stand out from the gray traffic.

You can choose a color from our color palette or we will develop an author's design for you: we make a print of any complexity and the most incredible imagination.

The film can be safely removed at any time, does not leave streaks, stains and does not damage factory paint

PROTECTIVE

We will protect and preserve the appearance of the car.

The anti-gravel film saves the paintwork from sliding scratches and branches, takes small shocks from sand, gravel and aggressive chemicals on the road.

As long as there is film on the machine, the surface remains new, as before pasting.
